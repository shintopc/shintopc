POLICY

Last updated: October 2024. This document outlines how ShintoPC Labs collects, uses, and protects your information across our mobile applications and services.

database

Information Collection

We only collect information that is absolutely necessary to provide our services. For apps like Kinetix Speedometer, this may include temporary location data which is processed entirely on-device to calculate speed and distance. For UPI Voice Alert, we parse notification data locally. We do not transmit sensitive personal data to external servers without explicit consent.

  • check_circle Location Data: Accessed only while the app is active for GPS tracking features. Not stored or sold permanently.
  • check_circle Notification Access: Used solely to generate voice alerts locally. Audio generation is processed without sending notification text off-device.
memory

How We Use Data

The data collected is used strictly for internal application functionality and crash reporting. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing arrays.

security

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect against unauthorized access or alteration of your information. All analytics logs are anonymized and stored using modern encryption standards.
